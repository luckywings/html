Bigil was released in 2019, directed by Atlee, is a football action film. Vijay underwent "special training" for his role in the film.[106] Bigil was Vijay's last film of the decade. Bigil received mixed reviews from critics, but Vijay was praised for the portrayal of his characters within the film.[107] It was a commercial success, collecting more than ₹300 crore,[108] became both the highest-grossing Tamil film of 2019—within three months of its release—and the highest-grossing film of Vijay's career. Bigil was also the first Tamil film released in Egypt and Jordan.[109] In 2020, Bigil was the first Tamil film re-released in Germany

Vijay married Sangeetha Sornalingam,[150][151] a Sri Lankan Tamil whom he met in the United Kingdom, on 25 August 1999.[152][153] They have two children.[154] Vijay's son made a cameo appearance with his father in Vettaikaaran (2009) and his daughter portrayed a small role as her father's pre-teen daughter in Theri (2016).

On 13 July 2021, the Madras High Court dismissed a writ petition filed by Vijay in 2012 seeking exemption of the Entry Tax for his Rolls-Royce Ghost car that was imported from England. It imposed a fine of ₹1 lakh which was allotted to the Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Public COVID relief fund. Justice S.M. Subramaniam that included the critical remarks and also stayed the order of a ₹1 lakh fine amount.[163] On 25 January 2022, the court dismissed and removed the defamatory critical statements made by Judge S.M.
